Taming a Bear Market in Retirement Income Calculator

If you’re saving for retirement, you’re probably used to seeing the value of your retirement income going up and down with the financial markets. However, once you retire, this may be a greater concern.


Taking withdrawals from your retirement accounts during market downturns can significantly reduce their value over the long term. Taming a Bear Market in Retirement Income strategy will help your clients better understand the impact that market volatility can have on their retirement assets. Our calculator will maximize your retirement income and never run of your money. Assume that the investment results mirror the annual historical returns of the S&P 500 Index (1926-2020).
